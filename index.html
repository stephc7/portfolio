Quality Assurance

I work to ensure software is reliable, functional, and performs as expected. My focus is on spotting potential issues early, improving processes, and delivering quality products that meet user expectations. I believe QA is not just about finding bugs but about creating a better overall experience.

Manual Testing

I specialize in manual testing to ensure every part of the software works as it should. By testing features and workflows from a user’s perspective, I can catch issues that automated tools might miss. It’s all about making sure the product feels intuitive and works smoothly for its users.

Automated Testing

I use tools like Robot Framework and Selenium to make the testing process faster and more efficient. By automating repetitive tasks, I help save time and improve accuracy. I focus on creating flexible and reliable tests that can adapt to the project’s needs and ensure everything runs as expected.
